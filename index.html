Reflection

Overall, I’m really happy I chose to learn blender for this special topics course. Even though Natasha and I didn’t create a high fidelity prototype. We got to learn a lot about our respective softwares. For me specifically, I will be practicing my modelling skills in the summer. I thoroughly enjoyed creating my silly animals. I learned how to create basic to more advanced shapes, UV map, being mindful of lighting, as well as how to colour 3D models. Going forward I want to implement my 3D models into my thesis. Or, creating 3D models to demonstrate real life models/prototypes of items. Mainly to place in my portfolio. So the user has a better view of the 3D model without relying solely on images.
